Case Study

Description

Designed exclusively for school students, this event assigns names of defunct companies. Participants must prepare a report and presentation detailing the company's story and case study for evaluation by jury members and the audience.

Rules of Procedure

  • Students will be notified about the company allotted to them following the registration.
  • students will prepare a presentation which they need to present before the judges.
  • students will be judged on the basis of various criterias like research, presentation,fluency and style of delivery.
  • A question hour sessionn will follow after the presentation round.

Guidelines

  • The registration will be based on first come first serve basis.
  • a team must consist of 2-3 members.
  • students must maintain the decorum during the event.
  • facts and details in the presentation should be authentic and correct.
  • Students must adhere to the timings of the event.
  • students must follow the format provided to them by the organizing committee.

Details

Date: 29th Nov - 1st Dec

Location: Silicon Univeristy
