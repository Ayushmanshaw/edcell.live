Panel Discussion

Description

Panel Discussion is a forum that features alumni and keynote speakers discussing entrepreneurship, startups, and their personal journeys. It provides students with a chance to interact with industry leaders.

Guidelines

  • Respectful Engagement: Decorum of the event must be maintained.
  • Focus on the topic: Discussions should remain focused on corporate success and failure based on the agenda only.
  • Constructive Questions: audience can ask Questions to the panel based on the ageda and discussion.
  • Case Study Reference: Ensure any examples are relevant and supported by facts.
  • Time Management: the time limit and deadlines should be respected throughout the event.
  • Networking Etiquette: Introduce yourself and engage in respectful conversations during breaks.
  • Inclusivity: Encourage diverse perspectives and create a welcoming environment for all attendees

Details

Date: 29th Nov - 1st Dec

Location: Silicon Univeristy
