Panel Discussion

Description

This forum features alumni and keynote speakers discussing entrepreneurship, startups, and their personal journeys. It provides students with a chance to interact with industry leaders, conducted in both online and offline formats.

Guidelines [Offline]

  • Respectful Engagement: Engage with respect; personal attacks and harassment will not be tolerated.
  • Focus on the topic: Discussions should remain focused on corporate success and failure.
  • Constructive Questions: Ask thoughtful questions that promote dialogue and learning.
  • Case Study Reference: Ensure any examples are relevant and supported by facts.
  • Time Management: Adhere to speaking and questioning time limits to allow for full participation. etc.
  • Networking Etiquette: Introduce yourself and engage in respectful conversations during breaks.
  • Inclusivity: Encourage diverse perspectives and create a welcoming environment for all attendees

Guidelines [Online]

  • Respectful Engagement: Engage with respect; personal attacks and harassment will not be tolerated.
  • Focus on the topic: Discussions should remain focused on corporate success and failure.
  • Constructive Questions: Use the chat feature to ask thoughtful questions that foster dialogue.
  • Case Study Reference: Ensure any examples are relevant and supported by facts.
  • Time Management: Adhere to speaking and questioning time limits to allow for full participation. etc.
  • Online Etiquette: Mute your microphone when not speaking, and use video when possible to enhance engagement.
