Bidding War

Description

Bidding War is a strategic team game that challenges your business acumen. Participants will use negotiation skills, strategic thinking, and teamwork to acquire valuable properties through competitive bidding. The game consists of multiple bidding rounds, where teams must assess the value of properties and make informed decisions. At the end of the game, the team with the highest number of items and cash count will emerge victorious.

Rules of Procedure

  • The event begins with the assignment of countries to different teams, with each team taking their position according to the designated country. .
  • Based on prior discussions, the OC will release the auction board, listing the products from each respective country along with their price ranges. No changes to the prices or points will be permitted once the event begins.
  • Participating teams will bid on the products following the standard auction process set by the organizing committee prior to the event.
  • Each team will be allocated an equal amount in their treasury, which they can utilize according to their strategy and interests. Each product will have a fixed price, and any team interested in purchasing it must spend the corresponding amount from its Treasury. If a team attempts to bid without sufficient funds, it will be disqualified from the event.
  • Each product will carry specific points, which will contribute to determining the winning team. The final score will be calculated by summing the remaining amount in the Treasury and the points earned through the auction.
  • The entire event will be monitored by the jury, led by the auction coordinator.

Guidelines

  • Bidding Wars is a team-based game where each team may consist of up to 4 participants.
  • The registration fee for Bidding Wars is INR 50 per participant.The registration will be based on a first come, first served basis.
  • Once the event starts, neither the prices nor the points will change.
  • No discussions among teams will be allowed during the event, and no outsiders will be permitted in the bidding room during the auction.
  • If a team attempts to bid without sufficient funds, it will be disqualified from the event.
  • Bidding Wars simulates an auction-friendly environment. Heated debates and arguments among participants or with jury members will not be tolerated.
