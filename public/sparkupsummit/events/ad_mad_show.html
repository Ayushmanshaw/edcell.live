Ad Mad Show

Description

Ad-mad Show is a national-level online event where students from all across India participate in creating advertisements and presenting them. Participants will have a chance to select brands in advance out of the multiple brands assigned to them by the organizing committee and prepare videos showcasing their creativity and videography skills. The ads and presentation will be judged by a panel that will select the best submissions.

Rules of Procedure

  • Team Composition: Teams should consist of 4-5 members.
  • Eligibility: Open to teams from all colleges across India.
  • Event Structure: Each team will be given 4 brand options and must select one to create a 60-second advertisement video and prepare a presentation explaining the brand and ad concept.
  • Submission: Teams must submit the ad video (MP4, max 200 MB) and presentation (PPT) by the given deadline.
  • Final Presentation: Teams will present their ad and concept in a 6-minute online session via Google Meet or Zoom, followed by a brief Q&A with judges.
  • Judging Criteria: Creativity,brand relevance, clarity and presentation skills.

Guidelines

  • Registration:The registration fee per participant is INR 50. The registration will be based on a first come, first served basis.
  • Content Development:Ads should be creative, engaging, and align with the brand’s message within the 60-second limit.
  • Concept & Strategy: Ensure the ad is practical, impactful, and effectively communicates the brand’s value.
  • Time Management: Manage your time wisely to complete both the ad and presentation on time.
  • Presentation Preparation: Prepare a clear, concise pitch that explains your ad concept and strategy, using visual aids to enhance your presentation.
  • Requirements: All content must be original, ethical, and plagiarism-free. No team is allowed to use any content against a rival brand in their ad or presentation.
