Ideathon

Description

This event invites students from various institutes to showcase their innovative ideas through presentations. Participants will select their own problem statements in advance and must provide effective solutions during the competition. Ideathon will take place during the three days of the SPARKUP Summit. The students will provide their innovative solutions through a provided template for presentation.

Rules of Procedure

  • The ideathon involves a presentation round where participants present their ideas in order to find solutions to day-to-day problems.
  • Each team will be assigned a specific time slot for their presentation, consisting of a 6-minute presentation followed by a 4-minute question-and-answer session.
  • A proper format of presentation will be followed during the pitch.
  • Strict adherence to the time limit is to be followed by the participants.
  • The top ideas will be recognized and awarded based on the judgement of the jury and panelists.

Guidelines

  • Each team in IdeaThon can consist of up to 3-5 participants.
  • The registration fee per participant is INR 50. The registration will be based on a first come, first served basis.
  • Teams must present their ideas strictly in the format specified by the organizing committee (OC). Presenting prototypes is optional and under the discretion of the team.
  • Once submitted, no changes to the presentation will be permitted.
  • The top ideas will be recognized and awarded based on the judgement of the jury and panelists.
