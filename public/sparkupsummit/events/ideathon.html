Ideathon

Description

Ideathon is a idea presentation event where they adddress different problem statements and provide solutions to it through different ideas.

Rules of Procedure

  • Ideathon involves a presentation round where participants present their ideas in order to solutions to problems related to day-to-day problems.
  • Each team will be assigned a specific time slot for their presentation, consisting of a 6-minute presentation followed by a 4-minute question-and answer session..
  • A proper format of presentation will be followed during the Pitch.
  • Strict adherence to the time limit is to be followed by the participants.
  • The top ideas will be recognised and awarded based on the judgement of the jury and panellists.

Guidelines

  • Each team in IdeaThon can consist of up to 3-5 participants.
  • The registration fee per participant is INR 50.The registration will be based on first come first serve basis.
  • Teams must present their ideas strictly in the format specified by the organising committee (OC).Presenting prototypes is optional, and under the discretion of the team.
  • Once submitted, no changes to the presentation will be permitted.
  • The top ideas will be recognised and awarded based on the judgement of the jury and panellists.
